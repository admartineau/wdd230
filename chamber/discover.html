A Culture of Community

About Our Fair City

Littleton is south west of Denver Colorado. It is located in Arapahoe, Douglas, and Jefferson counties, Colorado, United States. Littleton is the county seat of Arapahoe County and is a suburb of the Denver metropolitan area. The city population was 45,652 at the 2020 United States Census, ranking as the 20th most populous municipality in the State of Colorado.

Historic Main Street

The Downtown Littleton district has been a vibrant community hub for over 100 years. Come enjoy the many merchants, restaurants and service providers that make up our neighborhood today.

Events

There is always something happening in Littleton. From our world famous Western Welcome Week in August, to block parties every weekend in the summer months, to holiday shopping and celebrations, and arts festivals year round that celebrate our local talent.

Red Rocks Amphitheatre

Red Rocks Amphitheatre (also colloquially as simply Red Rocks) is an open-air amphitheatre built into a rock structure in the western United States, near Morrison, Colorado, just outside of Littleton. There is a large, tilted, disc-shaped rock behind the stage, a huge vertical rock angled outwards from stage right, several large outcrops angled outwards from stage left and a seating area for up to 9,525.

Come to Play

Colorado is know for its outdoor life. With easy access to the mountians in the west and the plains in the east, Littleton offers the ability to choose a wide variety of activities all year round.

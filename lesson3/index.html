Site Purpose

The purpose of the Littleton Chamber of Commerce site is to serve businesses in the community by empowering them to work with other businesses to create a vibrant economy and community.

The site will contain pages dedicated to chamber members and membership, economic development, community events, public policy and business advocacy, and chamber programs.

Target Audience and Scenarios

The primary audience for our Chamber of Commerce site is our members. The site will provide resources for our members to help them successfully navigate the economic and social challenges of the day. This includes events and programs to meet with other local businesses in open forums that encourage open discussion of relevant topics, advocacy, and government initiatives..

The secondary target audience for our Chamber of Commerce site is the local businesses and professionals that are potential members of the chamber. We look to use the site to get to know our community and invite new businesses and talent to join our cause.

Color Scheme and Typography

Our color scheme will be in the blue ranges, denoting dependability and reassurance. The typography will be the Montserrat font family.
